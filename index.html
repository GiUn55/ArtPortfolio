HIGHLIGHTED WORKS

A4, Graphic pencils (HB, 2H, 3B, 8B, 10B)

The Veiled Virgin

The famous sculpture The Veiled Virgin is reinvented in this artwork with careful consideration for shape, light, and emotion. The delicate, accurate shading used to depict the highlights and the ethereal appearance of a translucent veil covering her face. The underlying facial features are still discernible despite the fabric's weight and opacity, demonstrating the interaction between spirituality and realism.

A4, Oil colors

The rose forest

This oil painting presents a dreamlike forest landscape where natural beauty and quiet symbolism intertwine. Rich, layered brushstrokes build a lush woodland setting, with deep greens and earthy tones creating a sense of depth and mystery. Light filters through the canopy in soft, warm glimmers, guiding the viewer’s eye toward the central figure of the scene — a striking blossom tree in full bloom.
